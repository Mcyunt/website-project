Guide: How to Set Up Your Server

Create an Account:

Go to [Website Closed] and sign up for a free account.

Access the Dashboard:

Once logged in, head over to your user dashboard where you'll find server creation options.

Deploy a Server:

Click on "Create Server", then choose your desired game (e.g., Minecraft, Rust, CSGO, etc.).

Configure Your Server:

Set your server name, location, RAM/CPU, and any additional mods or plugins you want to install.

Start Your Server:

Once configured, hit "Deploy". Your server will spin up within minutes.

Manage & Monitor:

Use the control panel to manage files, restart or stop the server, and monitor performance.

Share & Play:

Invite your friends using the IP address shown in your dashboard and start playing!

If you need help, reach out via our Contact section or check our full documentation.
